Our 5-Minute De-Stress Tips

These simple, actionable routines can be done anywhere in lecture room, in exams or before a pressureful engagement

The 4-Minute Deep Breathing Technique

Kindly follow these steps in order to rest your nervous system

  1. Find a comfortable and calm area and gently close your eyes
  2. Inhale slowly and deeply through your nose for 4 seconds
  3. Hold your breath gently for another 4 seconds
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds releasing all tension
  5. Repeat the whole cycle doing the Inhale, Hold, Exhale process 5 times

Quick Hydration and Fuel Tips

  • Keep the water bottle visible: like placing it on your desk where you can easily access it
  • Power snack: Try carrying a simple snack like nuts to prevent energy crashes
  • Mini-breaks: Try taking mini breaks when stressful
  • Eat foods rich in minerals
